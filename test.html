BIOGRAPHY

Early Life

I was born one year after the Battle of Endor to negligent parents who, despite not being around for most of my life, sure acted like they had a right to tell me who I really am, when they don't. They're not a problem anymore though. Parents: can't live with 'em, can't live without 'em, am I right? Except I can. I can live without them.

I went through rigorous training, honing my combat skills as well as my use of the Force. I even learned how to make my own lightsaber. Eventually I emerged from the darkness of my formative years and became the Supreme Leader of the First Order.

Life at Lehigh

After a brief and disastrous time spent learning from my loser scrub of an uncle, I decided to come to Lehigh to develop the skills needed to achieve my dreams: destroying the Resistance and ruling everything. The coursework is also a nice distraction from the constant angst and turmoil I feel raging inside of me.
