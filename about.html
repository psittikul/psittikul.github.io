ABOUT ME

I am a software developer with a passion for doing good and helping others - and I strongly believe that love and compassion should drive the work I do. I feel most inspired and challenged by projects where I can apply my skills to help others, and try to approach all problems with this philosophy in mind. Most of my work experience is with small, often non-profit organizations with limited budgets and/or resources, so I am adept in conceiving creative, customized, and accommodating solutions. My main areas of interest are full-stack development, user experience, and human computer interaction.

In my spare time I like to make web apps that use art to create a fun, imaginative experience. My other hobbies include Muay Thai, video games, digital illustration, and playing with my two cats.
