LINC Portal

User portal for LINC, a Lehigh Valley non-profit that provides support to over 400 individuals from companies such as Air Products, B. Braun, and Lutron Electronics. Design based on mockups by Amanda Grant.

SCOPE

LINC Portal

AUDIENCE
  • Newly relocated hires in need of assistance relocating and acclimating to the Lehigh Valley, referred by partner companies
  • HR representatives and recruiters from partner companies such as Air Products, B. Braun, Crayola, and Lutron Electronics.
BACKGROUND

The LINC Portal has two sides: an "employer side" for partner companies, and a "client side" for newly relocated hires.

On the employer side, hiring representatives from partner companies can refer new hires for LINC services, using the online referral form. All referral form submissions are stored in the WordPress database, allowing for easy, ongoing reporting on metrics such as cost and services utilized.

On the client side, new hires referred by partner companies can access the resources and information necessary for a smooth transition to their new home.

LINC Resource Web App & Database

BACKGROUND

I first conceived this web app upon seeing a critical issue that had been hindering LINC's efficiency since its founding: providing community resources to clients is at the heart of LINC services, but without a database or consistent formatting tool, LINC client coordinators relied on storing information in various spreadsheets and presenting deliverables to clients in poorly formatted text documents. This lack of necessary tools resulted in an exorbitant amount of hours spent searching for and often recreating existing content.

The design of the underlying database required acute examination and attention to detail. Being one of two employees who have been with LINC since its founding, I

As LINC client coordinators have a great deal of tacit knowledge regarding their work and are accustomed to carrying out their operations without the use of such technological tools, an iterative design process was necessary to ensure an intuitive and efficient user experience.

Topicalizer Data Visualization

AUDIENCE
  • Academics and researchers
  • Parents and caretakers of autistic children
BACKGROUND

The "Topicalizer" is a data visualization tool designed to represent recurring topics within large bodies (over 40,000 documents) of text-based content, as well as each topic's prevalence and coherence. Currently this tool is being used on the following data: journal entries by Bohemian poet Thom Gunn, and blog entries written by parents of autistic children.
